Internal demo for Kaiser Permanente to address nurse burnout and missed care opportunities.

- **nexonurse-v3.html** - Enterprise Edition (Latest)
  - Organizational hierarchy (Unit → Service Line → Region → System)
  - Executive dashboard with heat maps
  - Areas of concern and success stories

  - Monthly compliance checklist
  - Productivity reporting
